COMPUTER ENGINEERING STUDENT SITE

Welcome STEM Enthusiasts!

This site was created by computer engineering students as a resource for students to succeed in this program and ultimately in STEM overall.

Entering STEM is exhilarating and we want to help you succeed and learn to face the many challenges you may encounter in technology and engineering! CRTC Computer Engineering will teach you the basics of the engineering process, robotics, manufacturing, computer science, cybersecurity, web design, and more! These skills are instrumental to being a successfull professional and provide the perfect launchpad for a successful education or career.

Because there is so much that goes into this path, including technical and professional skills, this website will function as a sort of 'home base' for technical and professional resources and student support.
STEM is a community of creative and technical minds coming together to create innovative products and solutions. Many diverse perspectives help us grow to become the best students and professionals possible. This is why we work to make sure everyone feels that they can have a home in this classroom and in STEM as a whole. The power of belonging and understanding cannot be understated in such an instrumental field with such innovative and challenging content.

Community and collaboration are key! We are always working towards the most supportive, inclusive, and creative community possible.


Committed to Inclusive Excellence

The Students of CRTC's Computer Engineering program are commited to the fostering not just creativity and technical skills, but a sense of belonging for all students. We know that diversity strengthens our communities and are committed to making sure our classroom is a place where any student can learn, joke, collaborate, and ultimately grow into a successful professional.
